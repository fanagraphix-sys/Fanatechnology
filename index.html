Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a website take?
A standard 5-page website typically takes 1-2 weeks. Custom or premium websites with advanced features may take 3-4 weeks depending on the complexity of the design and integration.
Do you provide hosting?
Yes! All our website packages include domain and secure hosting for the first year, ensuring a hassle-free launch for your business.
Is SEO included?
Yes, basic SEO setup is included in the standard package, while higher tier packages include On-Page SEO and SEO-ready architecture to help you rank better on search engines.
Can I update the website myself?
Absolutely. We build most of our websites with an easy-to-use Admin Panel (CMS like WordPress or custom backend) allowing you to manage content, images, and products easily.
Do you offer support after launch?
Yes, we provide lifetime technical guidance and premium support packages to ensure your digital product stays updated, secure, and performs optimally.
